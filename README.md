# About

Athena LMS is a modern Learning Management System (LMS) designed to empower educators and engage learners. <br>
Tutors can upload, and sell their courses, while students enjoy a seamless experience to purchase, watch, and master new skills, Athena where learning becomes fun!

# Requirements: 
CRUD Courses. (has difficulty, category, total course duration, total modules, popularity, price, rate) <br>
CRUD Modules. <br>
CRUD Vidoes. <br>
CRUD Subscribtion/Private subscribtion. <br>
(Each course can contain many modules, each module can contain many videos). <br>
CRUD Rate a course with opinion (Opinion is optional). (After the student is subscribed to it, the rate of a tutor is calculated by the average rate of all their courses). <br>
Follow tutor. <br>
Add course to watch later. <br>
Add bookmark to a certain second in video. <br>
Search course with efficiently sorting . (Sorting by popularity, rate, price, and level (can be sorted by one or more attribute at the same time)). <br>
